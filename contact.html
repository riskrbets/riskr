Contact Us

Riskr is run by a small independent team. We would love to hear from you — whether you have feedback on the games, found a bug, want a leaderboard entry removed, or have an advertising enquiry.

The best way to reach us is by email:

riskrsupport@gmail.com

What to include

  • Leaderboard removal: tell us the display name and the approximate score/date so we can find and remove the entry.
  • Bug reports: let us know which game, your device and browser, and what happened.
  • General feedback: anything you'd like to see added or improved.

We aim to reply within a few business days. For details on how we handle your information, see our Privacy Policy.
