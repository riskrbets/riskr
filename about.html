About Riskr

Riskr is a free, browser-based arcade built around classic game-of-chance tables — Crash, Plinko, Slots, Blackjack, Roulette, and Craps. Everyone starts with the same $1,000 of pretend money, plays for fun, and races to post the biggest multiplier on a shared global leaderboard. The only thing you can actually win here is bragging rights.

This is not gambling. Riskr uses fake, in-game currency only. There is nothing to buy, no real money is ever wagered, deposited, or won, and no prizes of any real-world value are awarded. The game is intended purely as entertainment for adults (18+).

How it works

  • Your bankroll. Every run begins with $1,000 in play money. It lives only in your browser for the current session — refresh and you start fresh.
  • The tables. Switch between Crash, Plinko, Slots, Blackjack, Roulette, and Craps. Each is a self-contained mini-game with its own odds and payouts.
  • Cashing out. When you like your score, cash out to lock in your final multiplier (your bankroll ÷ $1,000) and optionally post it to the leaderboard under a display name of your choosing.
  • The leaderboard. Two boards are kept: an all-time board and a weekly board that resets every Monday. They rank the biggest multipliers cashed out by players around the world.

The games

Crash — a multiplier curve rises until it randomly "crashes." Cash out before it does to bank the multiplier; wait too long and you lose the bet.

Plinko — drop a ball through a field of pegs. The slots at the far edges pay the most; the middle pays the least.

Slots — spin the reels and match symbols for a payout.

Blackjack — get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.

Roulette — bet on where the ball lands: a number, a color, or a group.

Craps — roll two dice and bet the table: the pass line, the numbers, the field, and more.

Why we built it

Riskr is a hobby project — a love letter to the look and feel of a high-roller floor, rebuilt with zero stakes. It is meant to deliver the fun of a lucky streak and the friendly competition of a leaderboard without anyone ever risking a cent. If you enjoy chasing a high score, you are in the right place.
