Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Riskr ("we", "us", the "Site") handles information when you use our website at riskr.bet. We have tried to keep both the game and this policy simple: we collect as little as possible.

Information we collect

  • Leaderboard submissions. If you choose to post a score, we store the display name you type, your final multiplier, your fake-money amount, and a timestamp. Please do not enter your real name or any personal information as your display name — anything you submit is shown publicly to all players.
  • No accounts. There is no sign-up, login, or password. We do not ask for your email address, phone number, or any real financial information, because the game uses pretend money only.
  • Local storage. We use your browser's local storage to remember that you have acknowledged the age and "for fun only" notice, so we do not show it on every visit. This stays on your device.
  • Server logs. Our hosting and data providers (see below) may automatically log standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, and request times for security and reliability.

How we use information

We use the limited data above only to run the game: to display the global and weekly leaderboards, to keep the boards working and free of abuse, and to operate and secure the Site. We do not sell your information.

Cookies and advertising

We intend to display advertising on this Site through Google AdSense. To do this, third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising at www.aboutads.info.
  • For more information about how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

Until advertising is enabled, no advertising cookies are set by us. When ads are enabled, this section will already describe them so you can make an informed choice.

Third-party service providers

  • Vercel hosts this Site. See Vercel's privacy documentation for how they handle request data.
  • Upstash (Redis) stores the leaderboard entries described above.
  • Google Fonts is used to load the fonts you see; your browser requests these from Google, which may log the request.
  • Google AdSense (once enabled) serves advertising as described above.

Children

Riskr is intended for adults aged 18 and over and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Your choices and data removal

Because the game stores no personal account, the main data tied to you is any leaderboard entry you chose to post. If you would like an entry removed, contact us at riskrsupport@gmail.com with enough detail to identify it and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes — such as turning on advertising — will be reflected here before they take effect.
